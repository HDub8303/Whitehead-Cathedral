Our Story

More Than a Church — A Family

Established in 1953, Whitehead Cathedral is a Spirit-filled community rooted in faith, love, and the pursuit of God’s purpose. We believe every person who walks through our doors carries a divine calling.

Whether you’re a lifelong believer or exploring faith for the first time, you are welcome here. Our doors are open to all who seek truth, healing, and genuine community.

“A place where EVERYONE BELONGS, where you will find purpose and we will grow in Faith Together.”

Foundation

What We Believe

Our faith is grounded in the Word of God and the teachings of the Church of God in Christ. These core beliefs guide everything we do as a church family.

Learn More
✦
The Bible

We believe the Bible to be the inspired and only infallible written Word of God.

2 Timothy 3:16 • 2 Peter 1:21

✦
God

We believe that there is only One God, eternally existent in three persons: God the Father, God the Son and, God the Holy Spirit.

Deuteronomy 6:4 • Matthew 28:19

✦
The Blessed Hope

We believe in the blessed Hope, which is the rapture of the Church of God, which is in Christ, at His return.

Titus 2:13 • 1 Thessalonians 4:16-17

✦
Repentance

We believe that the only means of being cleansed from sin is through repentance and faith in the precious Blood of Jesus Christ.

Luke 24:47 • 1 Peter 1:18-19

✦
Salvation

We believe that regeneration by the Holy Ghost is absolutely essential for personal salvation.

John 3:5 • Titus 3:5

✦
Jesus Christ

We believe that the redemptive work of Christ on the Cross provides healing for the human body in answer to believing prayer.

Isaiah 53:5 • James 5:14-15

✦
The Holy Spirit

We believe that the Baptism of the Holy Spirit, according to Acts 2:4, is given to believers who ask for Him.

Acts 2:4 • Luke 11:13

✦
Sanctification

We believe in the sanctifying power of the Holy Spirit, by whose indwelling the Christian is enabled to live a holy and separated life in the present world.

Galatians 5:16 • 1 Peter 1:15-16
